Experiences

Through my time at Georgia Tech, I have garnered an interest in design. Through my newfound interest, I have taken steps to learn more and gain experience in the field.

I recently learned to create image layouts for magazines using other students' works of art. I have also had to chance to design websites and other branding images, as well as learning about the design and web development process.

Though I still have a long way to go, I have enjoyed what I have worked on so far and I hope to use these skills in the future to create better and improved works.
